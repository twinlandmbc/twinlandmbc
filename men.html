Our Mission

The mission of the Twinland Missionary Baptist Church Men’s Ministry Team is to encourage, equip, and energize the men of Twinland Missionary Baptist Church and our community to become all God has purposely designed each man to be through a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, discipleship, leadership, and obedience to the inerrant Word of God.

Activities

Our Men’s Ministry seeks to encourage men’s evangelism, discipleship, fellowship, and ministry projects through planning, coordinating, and promoting each of these activities in accordance with our mission statement.

Evangelism – through both outreach and in-reach proclaiming the good news of Jesus Christ to men
Discipleship – through Bible studies and small groups interactions
Fellowship – through planned events which bring men together
Ministry Projects – through organized outreach and in-reach projects designed to promote biblical truths
