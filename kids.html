About Our Kids!

Jesus said, "Suffer little children, and forbid them not, to come unto me: for of such is the kingdom of heaven."
- Matthew 19:14

The Children's Ministry at Twinland Missionary Baptist Church is anchored by this verse of scripture. Each program and event provided for our children is Bible-based and designed to help children learn about God and how to apply that knowledge to their lives. Each program is led by dedicated Christian teachers that realize that this responsibility is biblical. We have no higher priority than leading children to a closer relationship with Christ.

The Children's Ministry's purpose is to make church a place where children want to be!!! Our programs are exciting and fun while being instructive and inspirational for each child.

We believe that each parent (or guardian) is vital in this ministry to your child, so we welcome you to participate, observe and suggest.

Children’s Church 3/28/2020

Watch this Youtube video. It will cover a little of what we talked about last Sunday and new stuff for this Sunday. This video is 10 minutes long and talks about Joseph’s Journey.
After watching the video, have your child draw/color what they learned/remembered from the story. You can also ask questions about the video (provided below).
1. What did the brothers tell their dad?
-Joseph was killed by wild animals.
2. What actually happened to Joseph?
-Joseph was sold and became a slave.
3. Was Joseph a good slave?
-Yes, he was trusted by Potiphar and was promoted. Joseph was now in charge of everything that Potiphar owned.
4. Potiphar’s wife liked Joseph and wanted to kiss him. Did Joseph kiss her? Why?
-No, because he was a good man. He knew it was not right because she was married and Joseph was working for Potiphar.
5. When Joseph ran from Potiphar’s wife, what happened?
-She grabbed his clothes and lied. She told her husband (Potiphar) that Joseph tried to kiss her. Joseph was sent to jail and became head of the other prisoners.
6. What was the butler's dream?
-Three branches gave big grapes. He squeezed the grapes into a cup and gave it to the King (Pharaoh).
7. What was the baker's dream?
-Three baskets filled with bread. Birds came and ate all the bread.
8. What did Joseph say the dreams meant?
-In three days the butler would become Pharaoh’s (the King’s) butler again but the baker would be hanged.
9. Did the butler remember to tell Pharaoh (the King) about how Joseph can interpret dreams?
-He forgot until the Pharaoh (King) had a dream and no one knew how to interpret it. They took Joseph from prison and the Pharaoh (King) told him his dream.
10. What do you think is going to happen next?
-there is no correct answer to this. Just listen to what your child has to say and get them excited to find out what comes next, next Sunday.
